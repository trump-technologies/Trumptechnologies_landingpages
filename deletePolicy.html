Account and Data Deletion Policy

At Trump Technologies, we value your privacy and are committed to giving you full control over your personal data. If you no longer wish to use our services, you may request the deletion of your account and all associated data.

What will be deleted:

  • Your user account and login credentials
  • Personal information (e.g., email, name)
  • All usage and activity data associated with your account
  • Any other data stored or associated with your account in our systems

Processing Time:

All deletion requests will be processed within 7 business days of verification. You will receive a confirmation once the deletion is complete.

Important Notes:

  • Once deleted, data cannot be recovered.
  • Some minimal data may be retained if required by legal obligations (e.g., fraud prevention, tax laws).
